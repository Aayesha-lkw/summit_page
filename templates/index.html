About the Event

The AI and Data Summit 2025 is a one-day conference that brings together Data Scientists, DataOps engineers, and MLOps engineers to explore the forefront of innovation. From agentic and generative AI to breakthrough techniques, the summit provides a platform to discover emerging technologies, share expertise, and shape the future of autonomous and intelligent systems.
